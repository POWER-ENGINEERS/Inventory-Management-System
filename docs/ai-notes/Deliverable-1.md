### AI Response

Yes.

# Scope Review

The AI suggested avoiding unnecessary features that could increase development time.

Features removed from the scope:

- Online Payment Gateway
- SMS Notifications
- Mobile Application
- Barcode Printing
- Artificial Intelligence
- Multi-Branch Synchronization
- Cloud Synchronization

These features may be added in future versions.
